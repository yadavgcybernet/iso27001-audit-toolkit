## Purpose

This Statement of Applicability (SoA) is a mandatory document required by
ISO 27001:2022 Clause 6.1.3(d). It records all Annex A controls, states
whether each is included or excluded, provides justification, and references
implementation status.

This document must be kept current and reviewed whenever the ISMS scope,
risk assessment results, or business environment changes.

---

## Inclusion / exclusion key

| Symbol | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ✅ Included | Control is applicable and implemented (or in progress) |
| ❌ Excluded | Control is not applicable — justification required |
| 🔄 In Progress | Control is applicable; implementation underway |

**Important:** Excluded controls must have a documented justification.
The mere absence of a risk is not sufficient — the reason must be clearly stated.

## Important notes

- This SoA must link directly to the **Risk Treatment Plan** — every included
  control should map to at least one identified risk or a documented voluntary adoption.
- Excluded controls must have **written justification** — a certification auditor
  will specifically test that exclusions are reasonable and documented.
- This document must be **reviewed annually** at minimum and after any significant
  change to scope, infrastructure, regulations, or business operations.
- ISO 27001:2022 does not permit blanket exclusion of entire themes —
  each control must be individually assessed.
